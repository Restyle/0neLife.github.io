About the event

  • Knowledge sharing from current practitioners in outsourcing and product companies on how to develop a product mentality in your project teams. Real stories about solution oriented development processes, where every teammate cares about the quality and value of the product.

  • Duration 2 days, 10 hours one and consist 8 modules

  • After successful completion of the test, an accreditation certificate will be issued

  • Experienced data analysts and experts share useful metrics and latest data science technologies. Try new ways of decision making based on user feedback and clients complaints which are already transforming business and development processes all over the world.

  • Active IT people from popular IT communities and opinion leaders of USA will come to network and share tools that can be applied to your own challenges.

The title of the module #3

Nobody has a perfect life and all of us are faced with occasional injustice and variables. Rather than sitting there feeling pity for yourself, allowing it to get to you and providing you reason to make excuses, move ahead and put it behind you. Utilize it as motivation and use this time to grow and learn.

The title of the module #6

The greatest commodity all of us have is time, yet energy is just as crucial as time. Keeping physically active provides greater blood-flow to the brain, improved focus, and enhanced alertness. Make physical training a regular portion of your life and boost your opportunities at success.

The title of the module #7

Morals and principles are two things which sometimes seem nonexistent in our modern society, like a thing of the past. Identify which principles you have to guide your life through the rough times and the good times. Your morals and principles should not change, no matter what.
